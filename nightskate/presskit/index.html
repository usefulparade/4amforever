About the Game

Night Skate is an endless skate fantasy about girl with a skateboard and the world to herself. A single-button skating game that captures the weightless satisfaction of a massive ollie: press to launch, hold to float, release to slam back down.

Build creative combos in a chill endless runner that never speeds up. Improvise lines through procedural environments full of handcrafted pixel art.

About the Developer

Odd Animal is a small cooperative studio by siblings Luke and Clay Williams.

Luke does music, art, and code. He teaches game-making to middle schoolers and is the music editor for the podcast Reply All.

Clay does art, animation, sound design, and is an avid beatmaker.

This is our first game, but we've got big plans!
